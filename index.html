What is the JDM community?

The JDM community has been around for 10 years and helt many car meets around the world. There are over 30 thousand active members all around the world who all share one common thing, building JDM cars. JDM stands for "Japanese domestic market and refers to Japan's home market for vehicles. Japan has many of the most iconic street cars also known as "imports". In 1993 Japanese manufacturers released the fourth generation of the Toyota Supra which would be one of if not the most iconic car in the whole JDM scene. With its tough engine (2JZ) which stock would only put out around 200bhp but easily tunable and could sustain a lot of abuse would easily reach 600+BHP!
